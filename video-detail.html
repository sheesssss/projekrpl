How to Push Up

    For Beginners:
    • Reps per set: 5–10 push-ups.
    • Sets per session: 2–3 sets.
    • Rest between sets: 30–60 seconds.
    • Frequency: 3–4 times per week, with 1 day of rest in between for recovery.

Tip: Focus on maintaining proper form during push-ups to avoid injury. Keep your back straight and your elbows at a comfortable angle.
